Our mission at Open O1 is to empower the community with advanced open-source alternatives that match the powerful capabilities of proprietary models. We are committed to:

  1. Achieving o1-like Performance: We are dedicated to creating an open-source model that not only matches but also advances the performance levels seen in proprietary models, specifically in areas such as code generation, mathematical problem-solving, and effective tool usage.
  2. Exploring Diverse Applications: Our commitment includes exploring and expanding the applications of o1-like models across various fields, including mathematics, coding, agent behavior, and tool utilization, thereby unlocking new potential for AI technology.
  3. Promoting Accessibility and Collaboration: We provide an open platform for developers, researchers, and enthusiasts to collaborate, ensuring that cutting-edge AI tools and research insights are accessible to everyone, irrespective of their resource constraints or geographical location.
